The Challenge

The Main Issue: A National Challenge

A major obstacle to the "Makan Bergizi Gratis" program is the significant variation in food prices across Indonesia's provinces. This volatility poses a serious risk to the national budget (APBN), potentially leading to a deficit if not managed effectively.

Budget Strain

Risk of exceeding the APBN due to unpredictable food costs in different regions.

Inefficient Allocation

The Solution

Data-Driven Optimization with Machine Learning

We propose a Machine Learning clustering algorithm to group provinces with similar characteristics. By analyzing economic potential, farmland productivity, and weather patterns, we can identify optimal locations for targeted investment.
