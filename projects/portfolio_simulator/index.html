The Problem

Averages Lie.

Most retirement planning tools use simple linear projections (e.g., "Stocks return 10% per year"). This ignores volatility, market crashes, and the critical "sequence of returns" risk. A portfolio that averages 10% can still go to zero if you retire into a bear market.

Warning: Linear models underestimate failure risk by ~40%.

The Solution

Probabilistic Modeling.

QuantSim uses Geometric Brownian Motion with Jump Diffusion to simulate 10,000 unique market lifetimes. We model the chaotic nature of reality—including fat-tail crashes, asset correlations, and mean reversion—to show you the full distribution of possible outcomes.

Insight: Understand your "Risk of Ruin" before it happens.
